# Interactive-Chocolate-Sales-Dashboard
Excel analysis of 3282 Orders to Identify product performance, sales trends, and employee contributions to revenue.

                                                   Project Overview  
This dashboard focuses on product performance, sales trends, and employee contributions to revenue.
The goal was to understand which products and salespeople drive business success.

  Business Questions  
- Which products sell the most?  
- Who are the top-performing salespeople?  
- How do sales change over time?  
- Which countries contribute most to sales?  

  Dashboard Breakdown  

1. Total Revenue KPI  
This provides a quick summary of total sales performance.

2. Monthly Sales Trends  
This tracks how revenue changes over time to identify sales patterns.

3. Sales by Country  
This shows that out of the 6 contries,Australia has made the biggest revenuefor the business so far.

4. Top Performing Products  
This Highlights products with the highest number of units sold.

5. Top Performing Salesperson  
This also identifies which salespeople is contributing to business growth.

   Key Insights  
- A few of our products dominate total sales  
- Certain salespeople consistently are doing well with their jobs  
- Revenue distribution varies across countries  

  Business Value  
This dashboard helps management identify top-performing products and employees, making it easier to reward performance and optimize sales strategy.
